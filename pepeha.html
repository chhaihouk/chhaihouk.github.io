The Significance and Beauty of Pepeha

A Pepeha is a unique and sacred tradition originating from the indigenous Māori people of Aotearoa, the beautiful land known as New Zealand. It is a form of introduction that connects individuals to their ancestral roots, cultural identity, and the natural environment that surrounds them.
